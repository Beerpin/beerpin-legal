# Support

Need help with BeerPin? You're in the right place.

**Contact:** [support@beerpin.de](mailto:support@beerpin.de)

We aim to reply within 2 business days.

---

## Quick answers

### How do I rate a beer?

Open the **Beers** tab, search for the beer (or add it if it's not in the list), open it, and tap the stars. You can add a photo, a comment, and choose whether to pin the location to your personal map.

### Where do I find my beer map?

Tap the **Map** tab. Your pins are marked in gold, your friends' pins (if they have allowed map sharing) in another colour. Tap a pin to see what beer was rated there.

### How does the social feature work?

There are two ways to connect with other users:

1. **Friends** — mutual relationship. You send a request and once accepted, you can see each other's ratings and (optionally) their map.
2. **Follow** — one-way, only possible for users who have made their profile **public**. Their activity shows up in your feed.

### How do I block or report another user?

- **Block:** Open the user's profile → tap **⋯** at the top right → **Block user**. They will no longer be able to see your activity, and they will disappear from your feed.
- **Report:** Same menu → **Report user**. Choose a reason and optionally add details. For comments, tap **Report** next to the comment.

We review every report. Accounts that violate our rules will be removed.

### How do I delete my account?

For now, please email **support@beerpin.de** from the email address linked to your account. We will delete your account and all personal data within 30 days. An in-app deletion button is coming in a future release.

### Why is BeerPin 18+?

BeerPin is about alcoholic beverages. Apple and German law require that apps with alcohol-related content are restricted to adults. We verify your age during sign-up.

### I forgot my password

On the login screen, tap **Forgot password?** and enter your email. You will receive a link to reset it.

### Push notifications

You will receive notifications when:

- A friend request is sent or accepted
- A friend rates a beer or writes a diary entry
- Someone likes or comments on your ratings

You can disable all BeerPin notifications in your iPhone's **Settings → Notifications → BeerPin**.

### My data didn't sync

BeerPin syncs in real time when you are online. If something looks off, pull-to-refresh on the affected tab. If the problem persists, write to **support@beerpin.de** and include your username and what you tried.

## Bug reports & feedback

Found a bug or have a feature idea? E-mail us at **support@beerpin.de** with:

- What you did
- What you expected
- What happened instead
- The version number from **Profile → Settings**
